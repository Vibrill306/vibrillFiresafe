About Us

Vibrill Fire Safe is an ISO 9001:2008 certified firm, established in the year 2001. The company is engaged in manufacturing, supplying, exporting and service providing of a comprehensive assortment of Fire Protection Systems. The product range offered by us is inclusive of Fire Alarm System, Industrial Emergency Lights, Exit Signage, and Fire Alarm Panels. Our given items are planned by taking ideal quality information factors at our merchant end. Our products are designed using high-quality raw materials and components that are sourced from reliable vendors of the market. Moreover, we additionally give Fire Extinguisher Installation Service, Fire Extinguisher Refilling Service and Fire System Maintenance Service. We work with some of the most reputed names in the industry that are known for their high-quality standards. Some of our major clients include NTPC, BHEL, Tech Mahindra, DLF, Mcdonalds, Woodland, and Havells.
